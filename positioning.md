# Brand Prompt Compare — Positioning

## Positioning Statement

**For content strategists and marketing leaders at B2B companies competing on expertise,** Brand Prompt Compare is the first tool that monitors how AI models describe your brand in direct head-to-head comparisons against competitors. Unlike non-branded prompt tracking tools that chase vanity queries your CEO types in, Brand Prompt Compare focuses on the branded comparisons where buying decisions actually happen — the prompts where prospects put your name next to a competitor and ask AI to pick a winner. It shows you exactly where you're winning, where you're losing, whether the sources AI cites are real, and what content gaps to fix so you stop losing deals you never knew were happening.
